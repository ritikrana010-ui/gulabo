About Gulabroohscent LLP Co.

Founded by Mr. Sajjan Pal Singh and Mr. Narender Kaushik, Gulabroohscent LLP Co. is a luxurious perfume brand rooted in the timeless beauty of the rose. Our expertise lies in cultivating fresh blooms and transforming their natural fragrance into exquisite perfumes and colognes.

Located in the heart of West Delhi, we combine traditional distillation with modern artistry to create scents that reflect elegance and purity.
